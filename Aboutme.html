My Mission

A person walks into work, he is assigned three tasks which he must complete. He must create a couple lines of Java that tells a car to break at certain distances relative to speed using information read from a lidar sensor. His second task is to create a warning sign for screens on airplanes that let the passengers aware of dangerous flight conditions by calculating wind turbulance weather conditions etc. This warning symbol will alert passengers as early as 15 mins before the actual event. His third task is to create a sharing page for Google Docs where people can send documents like business plans or school project from computers as a text to any phone number. He completed all his tasks for the day. He saved thousand of lives, stopped chaos during flights, and helped thousands share their ideas and start new companies all in one day of coding. That is who I want to be.
